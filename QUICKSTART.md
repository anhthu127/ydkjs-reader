# Quick Start Guide - YDKJS Reader

Welcome to the YDKJS Reader! This guide will help you get started quickly.

## What is YDKJS Reader?

YDKJS Reader is a web application that helps you read the Vietnamese translation of "You Don't Know JS" books with features to track your progress and plan your reading schedule.

## Features at a Glance

### 📚 Reading
- Browse 4 books with multiple chapters
- Beautiful markdown rendering with code highlighting
- Navigate easily between chapters
- Estimated reading time for each chapter

### 📊 Track Progress
- Mark chapters as read/unread with one click
- See your progress for each book
- View overall completion statistics
- All progress saved automatically

### 📅 Plan Your Reading
- Set how many minutes you want to read daily
- Get a personalized reading schedule
- See when you'll finish all books
- Adjust your plan anytime

## Getting Started (3 Easy Steps)

### Step 1: Browse Books
1. Open the website
2. You'll see a list of available books
3. Click on any book to expand and see chapters

### Step 2: Start Reading
1. Click on any chapter to start reading
2. Use the "Đánh dấu đã đọc" button to mark it complete
3. Navigate with ← Previous / Next → buttons

### Step 3: Track Your Progress
1. Click "Tiến độ" in the navigation menu
2. See your reading statistics
3. View all chapters you've completed

### Your Data is Local
- Everything is saved in your browser
- No account needed
- No data sent to servers
- Works offline after first load

### Clearing Data
- "Xóa tiến độ" button removes all progress
- "Xóa kế hoạch" button removes your reading plan
- Clearing browser data resets everything

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I access this on multiple devices?**
A: Yes, but progress won't sync. Each device has its own local storage.

**Q: Will I lose my progress if I clear browser cache?**
A: Yes. Consider exporting your progress (feature coming soon).

**Q: Can I change my reading plan?**
A: Yes! Just create a new plan with different settings.

**Q: What if a chapter doesn't load?**
A: Refresh the page. If it persists, the markdown file might be missing.

**Q: Can I read offline?**
A: After first visit, the app works offline, but you need internet for initial load.
